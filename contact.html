About Paul

Paul Bone is a software engineer and computer scientist living in Melbourne Australia. His interests include programming languages, declarative programming, programming language implementation, parallelism, concurrency, alpine skiing and music (flute).

Paul earnt his Ph.D. in 2013 for his contributions to the Mercury programming language and mostly for its automatic parallelisation capability. Paul is now applying these skills to Plasma!
